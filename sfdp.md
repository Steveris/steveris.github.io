# Standard Field Deployment Package

> Issued to all **Field Agents** upon activation  
> Replacements or upgrades require approval from **Division Command** or must be requisitioned through **Logistics, Infrastructure and Facilities (LIF)**. Tampering, unauthorized modification, or neglect of any assigned equipment will result in disciplinary review.
