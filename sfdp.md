# Standard Field Deployment Package

> Issued to all **Field Agents** upon activation  
> Replacements or upgrades require approval from **Division Command** or requisitioned through **Logistics, Infrastructure and Facilities (LIF)**. Tampering, unauthorized modification, or neglect of any assigned equipment will result in disciplinary review.

| Ammunition | Dept. Issue | Sufficient for standard engagement. Limited silver alloy and iron core rounds included. Other variations may be provided. | <img src="/assets/images/bullet.png" class="field-kit-image"> |

<div class="notice">
Personnel are advised that standard field issue is intended to support identification, communication and basic enforcement activities.

Specialized equipment, anomalous materials, or experimental devices are not included unless explicitly authorized.
